# Rocket Mail
Rocket Mail is an infinite fun 2d game where the player is trying to deliver an important mail from earth to another planet in the universe by a rocket. In this adventurous journey the player need to control the rocket and avoid hitting on to another planets, asteroids and even UFOs!

## How to Play
- Press **W** or **↑** to move the rocket upward.
- Press **S** or **↓** to move the rocket downward.
- Avoid all incoming obstacles.
- Travel as far as possible, the distance travelled is the score.
- Try to beat your own highscore.
